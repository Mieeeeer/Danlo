ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Sta. Ana Central Elementary School (SACES), located in Davao City, is a public institution under DepEd Region XI that offers quality education from Kindergarten to Grade 6. With a strong commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, SACES engages students in various extracurricular and community programs, fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment for future-ready learners.
